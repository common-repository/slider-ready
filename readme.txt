== Description ==

Now it's easy to create responsive image and video slideshows with Ready! [Slider plugin](http://readyshoppingcart.com/product/wordpress-slider-plugin/ "WordPress Slider plugin"). Tons of professional slider templates and options. Create stunning responsive slideshow without programming skills with Slider Ready!

* Responsive image slider. Mobile Ready!
* Professional slideshow templates
* Intuitive and easy-to-use interface
* WPML Support (Multi-Language)
* Use video in the slider (PRO)

[youtube http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ScXfUPzvlf0]

Attract more visitors to your site or clients to your store by displaying your best products in the simplest and most effective way possible! All you have to worry about is which pictures to select and leave everything else to Ready! Slider.

This plugin allows uploading large photo galleries to your site in no time. You can combine the pictures from the slider with videos, customize the way they are displayed and use hundreds of professional templates, creating an unforgettable experience for everyone who visits your site.

Eye-catching slideshows on your WordPress site are now one step closer. Install the Ready! Slider plugin or any other [WordPress Gallery Plugin](http://readyshoppingcart.com/wordpress-gallery-plugin/ "WordPress Gallery plugin") and create unique slideshows from your photo and video galleries! Why? Because nothing can beat the effect a beautiful picture has on human mind!
